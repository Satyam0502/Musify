About MUSIFY



Introduction


Musify is the newly designed web page which is used to stream all new and evergreen Indian songs. The basic difference between MUSIFY and other streaming platform is that MUSIFY offers free music without any other ads in betweeen songs or any other place. And also it has a very user friendly interface which provides option for all it's users to upload their content in .mp3 format and have the opportunity to get the title of CROWN MUSIFY USER !
